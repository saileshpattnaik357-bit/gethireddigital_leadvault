# Business Context

LeadVaultAI is a standalone local lead-mining application for ICP-aware procurement intelligence.

It serves agencies, consultants, and service businesses that need to find real buyer intent rather than broad content mentions. The product exists to turn a client ICP, website, service list, positioning, and target audience into a mining brain that finds people actively looking for external help.

Primary use case:

- Generate client-specific buyer clusters, search queries, and exclusion filters.
- Mine procurement-style signals from LinkedIn, public web, and related sources.
- Export accepted leads in the existing workflow-compatible schema.

The project is currently built for the owner/user as a local working tool, with a longer-term path toward a multi-tenant SaaS and autonomous procurement operating system.
